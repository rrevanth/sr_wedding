Revoori

Wedding Invitation

Sharabesham and Vasantha's dear son, Revanth,

With Sravya, Vidyasagar and Rama's grant,

A new life's journey they have now begun,

Join us for joy, with love's sweet chant.

On November's twenty-second bright morn,

At eleven, a new joy will be born.

With turmeric blessings, life we adorn,

In yellow and green, let spirits be drawn.

Join us at 11:00 AM at the Pool Deck, Waltair Club, followed by lunch.

On the twenty-third, a sacred vow,

At ten fifty-eight, beneath the bough.

At Sai Priya Resorts, here and now,

Two souls unite, with a graceful bow.

The wedding ceremony at 10:58 AM at Sai Priya Resorts will be followed by lunch.

On the twenty-fifth, with hearts so true,

At ten-thirty, in skies of blue.

A sacred Vratham, fresh and new,

At Mango Meadows, for me and you.

The Sri Satyanarayana Vratham at 10:30 AM at Mango Meadows will be followed by lunch.

When evening stars begin to gleam,

At seven, we'll share a royal dream.

At The Crown, a joyful, flowing stream,

A dinner grand, a wondrous theme.

Join us at 7:00 PM at The Crown, Welcome Hotel, for dinner.

So come and join, with blessings bright,

And fill our days with love and light.

Your presence makes our future right,

A truly unforgettable sight.

We look forward to celebrating with you
